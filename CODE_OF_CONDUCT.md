# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity, expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and respect  
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  
- Being inclusive of differing viewpoints  

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or insults  
- Publishing others' private information  
- Unwelcome sexual attention  
- Any kind of threatening behavior  

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the Code of Conduct.  
They have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and communities.


## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction  
A private, written warning.

### 2. Warning  
A clear warning and explanation of consequences.

### 3. Temporary Ban  
Temporary removal from interactions.

### 4. Permanent Ban  
Permanent removal from the community.
